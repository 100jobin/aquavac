Our Service

Protect Your Water with Aquavac

Image Capture and Detection

The waste recognition system captures images using a Raspberry Pi camera, employing the YOLO algorithm to analyze and categorize waste in real-time video streams.

Autonomous Navigation

Equipped with GPS and a flight controller, the bot accurately navigates predetermined water bodies, with motor control managed by an Arduino Uno for precise movement.

Waste Segregation

Utilizing input from the YOLO algorithm, waste is sorted in the recycling chamber using an L grabber controlled by Arduino, ensuring efficient sorting processes.

Communication and Power

The system maintains communication via a Bluetooth module, while a 12V battery pack ensures uninterrupted operation in various environments.

Monitoring and Notifications

A comprehensive waste monitoring system tracks the amount of waste and water pollution levels, sending real-time alerts to connected smartphones for immediate action.

Bot Movement Control

DC motors facilitate the bot’s movement, allowing for agile navigation in challenging environments, further enhanced by the L298N motor driver for reliable operation.
